Our Story

Beauty as ceremony, not chore

Kuna's Beauty Salon — also known to longtime clients as KUSH Beauty Shop and Kushy's Beauty Haven — was founded by Inviolata Kuna Taaban out of a simple belief: every woman deserves an unhurried hour where she is the priority. What began as private appointments in a small Nairobi studio grew, by word of mouth, into a full beauty and wellness destination.

Inviolata trained across haircare, advanced skincare, and spa therapy, and built her practice around techniques that respect African hair textures and melanin-rich skin. Today, the salon brings that same attention to a wider community — pairing world-class technique with the warmth of a friend's living room.

  • Craft Precision technique, premium products, continual study.
  • Care Listening first. Pressure-free consultations, every visit.
  • Calm A quiet, sanctuary-like space — phones encouraged off.
  • Community Lifting Kenyan beauty professionals through mentorship.
